Who we are

Continental Consulting Limited is committed to tracking the international investment situation for Asia and the world. Accurately controlling the changes of market information, looking for the best investment opportunities worldwide as well providing our customers with a full range of information consulting services, including: investment management, industrial investment, investment information consulting, economic information consulting, business information consulting, market information consulting, marketing planning and other consulting services that our clients might need.

We focus on the capital operation in the international financial markets, and provide customers with first-class quality professional financial services. Continental Consulting Limited has its headquarters based in London as well as Hong Kong. The management of Continental Consulting Limited have a strong network of domestic and international relations and investment and financing experience and skills which we feel will most certainly be of a great advantage to our esteemed clients.

Our Mission

What we aim to do at Continental Consulting Limited is to use our numerous resources worldwide to cater to each individual client a strategy that works best for them. Our management team has lived in the Far East for over 15 years so we really can sense market trends and put together business opportunities that others might not be able to. We’re dedicated to fostering long-term relationships that allow us to continuously serve and support our clients. We stay attuned to both grassroots insights and top-level sources, understanding that valuable lessons can come from every corner. By staying connected to the pulse of the markets, economies, and financial industries, we gain a unique perspective that sets us apart from others in the field. Below is our 4-point contribution mechanism:

  • We create personalized strategies to align with each client’s unique goals.
  • Over 15 years in the Far East give us deep insights into local markets.
  • We identify emerging trends to unlock new business opportunities.
  • Our commitment ensures reliable advice and exceptional results.

Our Vision

At Continental Consulting Limited, our vision is to be a global leader in providing innovative investment and business solutions, empowering clients to thrive in dynamic markets. By harnessing our extensive experience and worldwide network, we aim to create sustainable growth opportunities and drive long-term success for businesses across the globe.
